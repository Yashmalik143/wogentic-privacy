Privacy Policy

App Name: wogentic.ai

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

wogentic.ai (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our application in connection with Instagram and Meta platform integrations.

1. Purpose of the Application

wogentic.ai is developed to test and enable automation workflows using Instagram APIs, including webhook event processing for messages, comments, and other interactions.

2. Information We Collect

  • Instagram account basic information (such as account ID and username)
  • Webhook event data (such as messages or comment events)
  • Technical logs for debugging and system performance

3. How We Use Information

The information is used solely for:

  • Processing webhook events from Instagram
  • Testing and improving application functionality
  • Monitoring and debugging system performance

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data except:

  • When required by law

5. Data Retention

Data received via webhooks may be temporarily stored for logging and testing purposes and is not retained longer than necessary.

6. Data Security

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

This application uses services provided by Meta Platforms, including Instagram APIs. Use of these services is subject to their respective terms and privacy policies.
