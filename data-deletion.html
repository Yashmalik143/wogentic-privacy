Data Deletion Instructions

App Name: wogentic.ai

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

wogentic.ai respects user privacy and provides a way for users to request deletion of their data.

1. How to Request Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data associated with wogentic.ai, you can request deletion by:

  • Sending an email to: support@wogentic.ai
  • Subject line: Data Deletion Request
  • Include your Instagram username or account ID

2. What Happens Next

  • We will review your request
  • We may verify your identity if required
  • We will delete your data within 7 business days
  • You will receive confirmation after deletion

3. What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Stored webhook data (messages, comments, logs)
  • Associated account identifiers

4. Data Retention Note

Some minimal data may be retained if required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes.

5. Contact

Email: support@wogentic.ai

This process complies with Meta Platforms policies.
